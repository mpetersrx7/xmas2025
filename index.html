4-Day Amsterdam Itinerary

Day 1: Historic Center & Canals
Quick Lunch
  • Broodje Bert
    Famous Dutch sandwiches
  • Winkel 43
    Apple pie and soup
Afternoon
  • Jordaan & 9 Straatjes
    Quaint canals and boutique shops
  • Anne Frank House
    Book weeks ahead!
  • Westerkerk Tower
    Indoor climb with city views
Evening
  • Covered Canal Cruise
    With glühwein - perfect winter activity
  • Moeders
    Dutch comfort food
  • Foodhallen
    Indoor food market
Day 2: Museums & Winter Vibes
Morning
  • Van Gogh Museum
    Pre-book tickets
  • Rijksmuseum
    Rembrandt, Vermeer - skip if short on time
Afternoon
  • The Avocado Show
    Healthy & insta-worthy
  • Black & Blue
    Cozy steakhouse
  • Vondelpark
    Winter walk if dry
Evening
  • Café 't Smalle
    Mulled wine at historic brown café
  • Restaurant Floreyn
    Modern Dutch cuisine
Day 3: Local Life & Fine Dining
Morning
  • Albert Cuyp Market
    Try stroopwafels & Dutch cheese
  • Heineken Experience
    If you like beer - otherwise skip
Afternoon
  • De Bakkerswinkel
    Quaint tearoom
  • Rembrandt House Museum
    Where he painted Night Watch
Evening
  • Restaurant Bougainville
    Michelin-starred, Dam Square views
  • De Kas
    Farm-to-table in a greenhouse
Day 4: Day Trip or Local Highlights
Option 1: Day Trip
  • Zaanse Schans
    Windmills + cheese tasting (20 min away)
  • Haarlem
    Medieval charm + Christmas markets (15 min by train)
Option 2: Amsterdam Highlights
  • A'DAM Lookout
    Indoor sky deck with heated swings
  • NDSM Wharf
    Hipster street art - take the free ferry
Evening
  • Café de Klos
    Best ribs in Amsterdam
  • Bistro Bar Spek
    Cozy French-Dutch cuisine

Tips for Your Amsterdam Trip:

  • Book Anne Frank House tickets weeks in advance
  • Consider purchasing a Museumkaart for museum discounts
  • Use public transportation (OV-chipkaart) or walk - Amsterdam is very walkable
  • Try traditional Dutch foods like stroopwafels, bitterballen, and herring
