Home / Amsterdam

Amsterdam

Explore the beautiful canals, museums, and vibrant neighborhoods of Amsterdam from December 13-17 (4 nights).

Points of Interest

View Interactive Map

Attractions

  • Anne Frank House
    Historic house and museum dedicated to Jewish wartime diarist Anne Frank
  • Van Gogh Museum
    Art museum dedicated to the works of Vincent van Gogh and his contemporaries
  • Rijksmuseum
    Dutch national museum dedicated to arts and history in Amsterdam
  • Zaanse Schans
    Neighborhood with historic windmills and houses
  • Albert Cuyp Market
    Famous street market with food, clothing, and local products
  • Amsterdam Central Station
    Historic railway station and main transportation hub

Restaurants

  • De Kas Restaurant
    Farm-to-table dining in a greenhouse setting
  • Restaurant MAX
    Modern European cuisine in a stylish setting. Visit website
  • Café de Jaren
    Popular cafe near the hotel with canal views and diverse menu

Hotels

  • INK Hotel Amsterdam
    Nieuwezijds Voorburgwal 67, 1012 RE Amsterdam, Netherlands

Suggested Itinerary (4 nights)

Confirmed Reservations:
  • Dec 14: Food tour of Jordaan at 5pm
  • Dec 15: Anne Frank House at 4:45pm
  • Dec 15: Dinner at De Kas at 7:45pm
  • Dec 16: Dinner at Restaurant MAX at 7:30pm

Day 1: December 13 - Arrival & Afternoon Exploration

Afternoon (Arrival around noon):
  • Arrive at Amsterdam Schiphol Airport and transfer to your hotel
  • Check into INK Hotel Amsterdam and settle in
  • Lunch at Café de Jaren (popular cafe near the hotel with canal views)
  • Take an afternoon stroll through the city center to get oriented
Evening:
  • Relax after travel and enjoy a casual dinner at Foodhallen (indoor food market with diverse options)
  • Evening walk along the illuminated canals near the hotel

Day 2: December 14 - Van Gogh Museum & Jordaan Food Tour

Morning:
  • Van Gogh Museum (allow 3 hours to fully appreciate the collection)
  • Explore the Museumplein area and see the iconic "I amsterdam" sign
Afternoon:
  • Quick lunch at museum cafe or nearby restaurant
  • Explore the 9 Straatjes (Nine Streets) for boutique shopping
  • Late afternoon exploration of the Jordaan district before the food tour
Evening:
  • 5:00 PM: Food tour of Jordaan (meet at designated location)
  • The food tour will include multiple tastings - perfect after exploring the neighborhood
  • After the tour, enjoy an evening walk along the illuminated canals

Day 3: December 15 - Rijksmuseum & De Kas Dinner

Morning:
  • Rijksmuseum (allow 3 hours to see Rembrandt's Night Watch and other masterpieces)
  • Explore the museum's extensive collection of Dutch art and history
Afternoon:
  • Lunch at De Bakkerswinkel (quaint tearoom) or a restaurant in the area
  • Waterlooplein Flea Market for vintage finds
  • 4:45 PM: Anne Frank House reservation (essential to book weeks in advance)
Evening:
  • 7:45 PM: Dinner reservation at De Kas (farm-to-table in a greenhouse)
  • Allow extra time for travel to the restaurant (located in Frankendael park)
  • After dinner, enjoy evening drinks at Café 't Smalle (historic brown café)

Day 4: December 16 - Albert Cuyp Market & Restaurant MAX

Morning:
  • Visit the famous Albert Cuyp Market (open Tuesday through Saturday)
  • Try local specialties like stroopwafels and Dutch cheese
Option 1: Day Trip to Zaanse Schans
  • Visit the historic windmills at Zaanse Schans (20 min train ride)
  • See traditional cheese-making and wooden shoe crafting
  • Return to Amsterdam for late afternoon
Option 2: Amsterdam Highlights
  • A'DAM Lookout with rooftop swing (indoor option in winter)
  • Take free ferry to NDSM Wharf for street art
  • Ice-skating at Museumplein (seasonal rink)
Evening:
  • 7:30 PM: Dinner reservation at Restaurant MAX (modern European cuisine)
  • Located in the city center - easily accessible from most areas
  • After dinner, enjoy a final evening walk along the illuminated canals

Day 5: December 17 - Departure

Morning:
  • Enjoy a final Dutch breakfast at your hotel or a local cafe
  • Last-minute souvenir shopping for Dutch specialties
  • Check out of hotel and transfer to airport for midday departure
Home / Lucerne

Lucerne

Explore the beautiful Swiss city of Lucerne from December 24-27 (3 nights).

Points of Interest

View Interactive Map

Attractions

  • Chapel Bridge
    Historic wooden bridge spanning the Reuss River
  • Lion Monument
    Rock relief commemorating Swiss Guards who died during the French Revolution
  • Mount Pilatus
    Mountain with panoramic views, accessible by the world's steepest cogwheel railway
  • Lake Lucerne
    Scenic lake surrounded by mountains, perfect for boat cruises
  • Old Town Lucerne
    Historic city center with medieval architecture and charming squares

Restaurants

  • Hotel Schweizerhof Luzern
    Elegant restaurant offering Mediterranean cuisine with Swiss influences
  • Restaurant Pfistern
    Traditional Swiss restaurant with fondue carriage experience

Hotels

  • Cascada Hotel
    Bundesplatz 18, 6003 Lucerne, Switzerland

Suggested Itinerary (3 nights)

Confirmed Reservations:
  • Dec 26: Fondue carriage at Restaurant Pfistern at 6pm

Day 1: December 24 - Christmas Eve Arrival

Evening (Arrival):
  • Arrive in Lucerne and check into Cascada Hotel
  • Take an evening stroll across the illuminated Chapel Bridge
  • Enjoy a special Christmas Eve dinner at Hotel Schweizerhof Luzern
  • Attend a Christmas service at Hofkirche (St. Leodegar Church) if desired

Day 2: December 25 - Christmas Day - Old Town & Lake

Morning:
  • Leisurely Christmas morning exploring the Old Town
  • Visit the Lion Monument and learn about its history
  • Enjoy the festive atmosphere in the quiet Christmas morning streets
Afternoon:
  • Scenic Lake Lucerne cruise (check for Christmas Day schedule)
  • Lunch at a restaurant that's open on Christmas (check in advance)
  • Explore the Musegg Wall and its towers
Evening:
  • Quiet dinner at your hotel or a restaurant open on Christmas
  • Evening walk along the lakeside promenade to see festive lights

Day 3: December 26 - Mountain Excursion & Fondue Carriage

Morning:
  • Ascend Mount Pilatus via the world's steepest cogwheel railway
  • Enjoy breathtaking panoramic views of the Swiss Alps
Afternoon:
  • Lunch at the mountain summit restaurant
  • Optional winter hiking or snow activities (weather permitting)
  • Descend via cable car for different perspectives
  • Return to Lucerne by late afternoon to prepare for evening reservation
Evening:
  • 6:00 PM: Fondue carriage reservation at Restaurant Pfistern
  • Allow time to find the restaurant location near the Chapel Bridge
  • Enjoy the unique horse-drawn carriage dining experience
  • After dinner, take a final evening stroll through the Old Town

Day 4: December 27 - Departure

Morning:
  • Final Swiss breakfast at your hotel or a local bakery
  • Last-minute souvenir shopping for Swiss chocolate and watches
  • Check out of hotel and transfer to station/airport for midday departure
Home / Vienna

Vienna

Explore the imperial city of Vienna from December 27 through January 2 (5 nights).

Points of Interest

View Interactive Map

Attractions

  • Schönbrunn Palace
    Former imperial summer residence with beautiful gardens
  • St. Stephen's Cathedral
    Gothic cathedral and one of Vienna's most recognizable symbols
  • Belvedere Palace
    Historic building complex with art museum and beautiful gardens
  • Vienna State Opera
    Historic opera house and one of the leading opera addresses in the world
  • Hofburg Palace
    Former imperial palace and current official residence of the President of Austria
  • Viennese Giant Ferris Wheel
    Iconic Ferris wheel at the entrance of the Prater amusement park

Restaurants

  • Café Central
    Historic Viennese coffee house with traditional pastries
  • Silvio Nickol
    Fine dining restaurant with two Michelin stars, offering modern European cuisine

Hotels

  • Hotel Sacher Wien
    Philharmoniker Str. 4, 1010 Wien, Austria

Suggested Itinerary (5 nights)

Confirmed Reservations:
  • Dec 27: Dinner at Silvio Nickol at 6:30pm

Day 1: December 27 - Arrival & Silvio Nickol Dinner

Afternoon (Arrival):
  • Arrive in Vienna and check into your hotel
  • Take an evening stroll around St. Stephen's Cathedral
  • Experience Vienna's famous Christmas markets (if still open)
Evening:
  • 6:30 PM: Dinner reservation at Silvio Nickol (two Michelin star restaurant)
  • Allow plenty of time to travel to the restaurant and prepare
  • Enjoy an exquisite fine dining experience to begin your Vienna journey

Day 2: December 28 - Imperial Palaces

Morning:
  • Visit Schönbrunn Palace, the former imperial summer residence
  • Explore the magnificent palace rooms and gardens
Afternoon:
  • Explore the Hofburg Palace complex
  • Visit the Sisi Museum and Imperial Apartments
  • See the Spanish Riding School (check performance schedule)
Evening:
  • Attend a classical concert or opera at the Vienna State Opera
  • Dinner at a restaurant in the Museum Quarter

Day 3: December 29 - Art & Culture

Morning:
  • Visit the Belvedere Palace to see Klimt's "The Kiss"
  • Explore the Upper and Lower Belvedere and their gardens
Afternoon:
  • Explore the Museum Quarter (Museumsquartier)
  • Visit the Leopold Museum or Kunsthistorisches Museum
  • Lunch at a nearby café
Evening:
  • Experience Vienna's coffee house culture at Café Central
  • Enjoy traditional Viennese pastries like Sachertorte
  • Evening stroll through the city center

Day 4: December 30 - Vienna's Markets & Prater

Morning:
  • Visit the Vienna Prater and ride the Giant Ferris Wheel
  • Explore the amusement park and surrounding area
Afternoon:
  • Tour St. Stephen's Cathedral and climb the South Tower
  • Explore the Vienna Ringstrasse by tram
  • Visit the Vienna City Hall and surrounding area
Evening:
  • Enjoy dinner at a traditional Viennese restaurant
  • Attend a performance or enjoy Vienna's vibrant nightlife

Day 5: December 31 - New Year's Eve

Morning:
  • Explore the Stadtpark with its famous Johann Strauss monument
  • Last-minute souvenir shopping for Mozartkugeln and other treats
Afternoon:
  • Prepare for New Year's Eve celebrations
  • Relax and enjoy a leisurely lunch
Evening (New Year's Eve):
  • Enjoy a special New Year's Eve dinner (book well in advance)
  • Join the celebrations at City Hall Square or along the Ringstrasse
  • Watch the fireworks at midnight

Day 6: January 1 - New Year's Day & Departure

Morning:
  • Leisurely morning recovering from New Year's Eve celebrations
  • Enjoy a final Viennese breakfast at your hotel or a local café
  • Check out of hotel and transfer to airport for midday departure
Home / Madrid

Madrid

Explore the vibrant capital of Spain from January 2 through January 5 (3 nights).

Points of Interest

View Interactive Map

Attractions

  • Museo Nacional del Prado
    Main Spanish national art museum, featuring European art from the 12th to early 20th century
  • Parque del Retiro
    Large park with a lake, rose garden, and the stunning Crystal Palace
  • Mercado San Miguel
    Popular indoor market with gourmet tapas, fresh produce, and drinks
  • Royal Basilica of Saint Francis the Great
    Historic basilica with a large dome and important religious artworks

Restaurants

  • The Botin
    World's oldest restaurant according to Guinness World Records, famous for suckling pig
  • La Bola
    Historic tavern famous for its traditional cocido madrileño (Madrid-style stew)
  • Restaurante Amicis
    Mediterranean and Italian cuisine in a cozy setting

Hotels

  • The Westin Palace, Madrid
    Plaza de las Cortes, 7, 28014 Madrid, Spain

Suggested Itinerary (3 nights)

Day 1: January 2 - Arrival & Evening in Madrid

Evening (Arrival):
  • Arrive in Madrid and check into The Westin Palace hotel
  • Take an evening stroll through the city center
  • Enjoy tapas and drinks at Mercado San Miguel
  • Dinner at Restaurante Amicis or a traditional Spanish restaurant

Day 2: January 3 - Art & Parks

Morning:
  • Explore the Museo Nacional del Prado to see works by Goya, Velázquez, and El Greco
  • Visit the nearby Botanical Garden
Afternoon:
  • Lunch at a restaurant near the museum or in the Retiro area
  • Spend the afternoon at Parque del Retiro, rowing on the lake
  • Visit the Palacio de Cristal glass palace in the park
Evening:
  • Enjoy traditional cocido madrileño at La Bola
  • Experience Madrid's nightlife in the Huertas or La Latina areas

Day 3: January 4 - Culture & The Botin

Morning:
  • Visit the Royal Basilica of Saint Francis the Great
  • Explore the historic city center and its architecture
Afternoon:
  • Lunch at a traditional Spanish restaurant
  • Explore the Royal Palace of Madrid and its beautiful gardens
  • Visit the Almudena Cathedral next to the palace
Evening:
  • Special dinner at the historic The Botin, famous for suckling pig
  • Evening stroll along Gran Vía to see the illuminated buildings
  • Optional flamenco show for a cultural experience

Day 4: January 5 - Departure

Morning:
  • Enjoy a final Spanish breakfast of churros with chocolate
  • Last-minute souvenir shopping for Spanish delicacies
  • Check out of hotel and transfer to airport for midday departure
