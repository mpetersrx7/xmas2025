Home / Amsterdam

Amsterdam

Explore the beautiful canals, museums, and vibrant neighborhoods of Amsterdam from December 13-17.

Points of Interest

  • Anne Frank House
    Historic house and museum dedicated to Jewish wartime diarist Anne Frank
  • Van Gogh Museum
    Art museum dedicated to the works of Vincent van Gogh and his contemporaries
  • Rijksmuseum
    Dutch national museum dedicated to arts and history in Amsterdam
  • Rembrandt House Museum
    Historic house and art museum where Rembrandt lived and worked
  • De Kas Restaurant
    Farm-to-table dining in a greenhouse setting
  • Restaurant MAX
    Modern European cuisine in a stylish setting. Visit website
  • Zaanse Schans
    Neighborhood with historic windmills and houses
  • De Pijp Neighborhood
    Vibrant district with market, cafes, and boutiques

Interactive Map

Suggested Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival, Check-In & Ease Into Amsterdam

Late Morning / Early Afternoon:
  • Arrive, check into hotel/Airbnb (drop bags if early)
  • Quick lunch at Broodje Bert (famous Dutch sandwiches) or Winkel 43 (apple pie + soup)
Afternoon:
  • Wander Jordaan & 9 Straatjes (quaint canals, boutique shops)
  • Anne Frank House (if you pre-booked tickets—essential to book weeks ahead!)
  • If no Anne Frank tickets: Westerkerk Tower (indoor climb with city views)
Evening:
  • Covered Canal Cruise (with glühwein—perfect winter activity!)
  • Casual dinner at Moeders (Dutch comfort food) or Foodhallen (indoor food market)

Day 2: Museums & Cozy Winter Vibes

Morning:
  • Van Gogh Museum (pre-booked tickets)
  • Rijksmuseum (Rembrandt, Vermeer—skip if short on time)
Afternoon:
  • Lunch at The Avocado Show (healthy & insta-worthy) or Black & Blue (cozy steakhouse)
  • Stedelijk Museum (modern art) or FOAM Photography Museum
  • Vondelpark winter walk (if dry) or Hortus Botanicus (tropical greenhouse escape)
Evening:
  • Mulled wine at Café 't Smalle (historic brown café)
  • Dinner at Restaurant MAX (modern European cuisine) or Restaurant Floreyn (modern Dutch)

Day 3: Local Life & Fancy Dinner

Morning:
  • Albert Cuyp Market (try stroopwafels & Dutch cheese)
  • Heineken Experience (if you like beer—otherwise skip)
Afternoon:
  • Lunch at De Bakkerswinkel (quaint tearoom) or Soup en Zo (warming soups)
  • Rembrandt House Museum (where he painted Night Watch)
  • Waterlooplein Flea Market (vintage finds)
Evening:
  • Fancy Dinner at Restaurant Bougainville (Michelin-starred, Dam Square views) OR
  • De Kas (farm-to-table in a greenhouse)

Day 4: Winter Day Trip or Festive Amsterdam

Option 1: Day Trip
  • Zaanse Schans (windmills + cheese tasting—20 min away)
  • Haarlem (medieval charm + Christmas markets—15 min by train)
Option 2: Amsterdam Highlights
  • A'DAM Lookout (indoor sky deck with heated swings)
  • NDSM Wharf (hipster street art—take the free ferry)
  • Ice-skating at Rembrandtplein (seasonal rink)
Evening:
  • Final dinner at Café de Klos (best ribs) or Bistro Bar Spek (cozy French-Dutch)

Food Tours

Consider these food tours to experience Amsterdam's culinary scene:

  • Eating Europe: Amsterdam Jordaan Food and Drinks Tour - Learn more
  • Eating Amsterdam: Albert Cuyp Market and the Pijp Food Tour - Learn more

Check availability and book in advance, especially during the holiday season.

Home / Lucerne

Lucerne

Explore the beautiful Swiss city of Lucerne from December 24-27.

Points of Interest

  • Chapel Bridge
    Historic wooden bridge spanning the Reuss River
  • Lion Monument
    Rock relief commemorating Swiss Guards who died during the French Revolution
  • Hotel Schweizerhof Luzern
    Luxurious 5-star hotel with stunning lake views
  • Fondue Carriage at Restaurant Pfistern
    Unique dining experience in a horse-drawn carriage. View details
  • Swiss Museum of Transport
    Large museum exhibiting all forms of transport including rail, air, and automotive
  • Mount Pilatus
    Mountain peak accessible by cable car with stunning views of the Alps

Interactive Map

Suggested Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Old Town Exploration

Morning:
  • Arrive in Lucerne and check into your hotel
  • Take a stroll along the Chapel Bridge and visit the Water Tower
Afternoon:
  • Visit the Lion Monument and learn about its history
  • Explore the Old Town with its picturesque squares and historic buildings
Evening:
  • Enjoy a traditional Swiss fondue dinner at Restaurant Pfistern
  • Take an evening walk along the lakefront

Day 2: Mountain Excursion

Morning:
  • Take a boat trip on Lake Lucerne
  • Ascend Mount Pilatus via the world's steepest cogwheel railway
Afternoon:
  • Enjoy lunch with a view at the mountain summit restaurant
  • Hike along the mountain trails (weather permitting)
Evening:
  • Return to Lucerne and relax at a local café
  • Dine at a restaurant with traditional Swiss cuisine

Day 3: Culture and Departure

Morning:
  • Visit the Swiss Museum of Transport
  • Explore the Bourbaki Panorama, a circular painting depicting the Franco-Prussian War
Afternoon:
  • Last-minute souvenir shopping in the old town
  • Enjoy a farewell lunch at a lakeside restaurant

Home / Innsbruck

Innsbruck

Explore the beautiful Austrian city of Innsbruck from December 27-29.

Points of Interest

  • Nordkette
    Mountain range accessible via cable car with stunning views of Innsbruck
  • Court Church
    Gothic church with the tomb of Emperor Maximilian I
  • Altstadt von Innsbruck
    Historic old town with colorful buildings and medieval architecture
  • Ambras Castle
    Renaissance castle and museum with beautiful gardens
  • Bergisel Ski Jump
    Olympic ski jump with panoramic views and a café designed by Zaha Hadid
  • Tyrolean State Museums
    Museum complex showcasing Tyrolean art, culture, and history

Interactive Map

Suggested Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Old Town Exploration

Morning:
  • Arrive in Innsbruck and check into your hotel
  • Take a walking tour of the Old Town, including the Golden Roof
Afternoon:
  • Visit the Court Church with its famous Black Men statues
  • Explore the Imperial Palace (Hofburg)
Evening:
  • Enjoy traditional Tyrolean cuisine at a local restaurant
  • Stroll through the Christmas markets (seasonal)

Day 2: Mountain Adventure

Morning:
  • Take the Nordkette Cable Car to the Top of Innsbruck
  • Enjoy breathtaking views of the Alps
Afternoon:
  • Visit the Bergisel Ski Jump and its panoramic café
  • Explore Ambras Castle and its collections
Evening:
  • Relax at a traditional Austrian beer hall
  • Enjoy dinner with views of the illuminated city

Day 3: Culture and Departure

Morning:
  • Visit the Tyrolean State Museums
  • Explore the Tyrol Panorama with its giant circular painting
Afternoon:
  • Last-minute souvenir shopping for local specialties
  • Enjoy a farewell lunch at a traditional Gasthaus

Home / Vienna

Vienna

Explore the beautiful Austrian capital of Vienna from December 29 to January 1.

Points of Interest

  • Schönbrunn Palace
    Former imperial summer residence with beautiful gardens
  • Belvedere Palace
    Historic building complex with art museum and beautiful gardens
  • St. Stephen's Cathedral
    Magnificent Gothic cathedral in the heart of Vienna
  • Hofburg
    Former imperial palace and current official residence of the Austrian president
  • Vienna State Opera
    Historic opera house and one of the busiest opera venues in the world
  • Viennese Giant Ferris Wheel
    Iconic ferris wheel in the Prater amusement park

Interactive Map

Suggested Itinerary

Day 1: Imperial Vienna

Morning:
  • Arrive in Vienna and check into your hotel
  • Visit Schönbrunn Palace and explore its magnificent gardens
Afternoon:
  • Explore the Hofburg Palace complex
  • Visit the Spanish Riding School (if performances are scheduled)
Evening:
  • Attend a classical concert or opera at the Vienna State Opera
  • Enjoy dinner at a traditional Viennese restaurant

Day 2: Art and Culture

Morning:
  • Visit the Belvedere Palace to see Klimt's "The Kiss"
  • Explore the Museum Quarter (Museumsquartier)
Afternoon:
  • Tour St. Stephen's Cathedral and climb the South Tower
  • Explore the Naschmarkt for lunch and local delicacies
Evening:
  • Experience Vienna's coffee house culture at Café Central
  • Take a evening stroll through the city center

Day 3: Parks and Departure

Morning:
  • Visit the Vienna Prater and ride the Giant Ferris Wheel
  • Explore the Stadtpark with its famous Johann Strauss monument
Afternoon:
  • Last-minute souvenir shopping for Mozartkugeln and other treats
  • Enjoy a farewell lunch at a Heuriger (traditional wine tavern)

Home / Madrid

Madrid

Explore the beautiful Spanish capital of Madrid from January 1-5.

Points of Interest

  • Parque del Retiro
    Beautiful park with gardens, monuments, and a large boating lake
  • Museo Nacional del Prado
    Spain's main national art museum, featuring European art from the 12th to early 20th century
  • Mercado San Miguel
    Iconic covered market with gourmet tapas and traditional Spanish products
  • Royal Palace of Madrid
    Official residence of the Spanish Royal Family with opulent interiors
  • Plaza Mayor
    Grand square in the heart of Madrid, surrounded by historic buildings
  • Reina Sofía Museum
    Modern art museum housing Picasso's famous Guernica painting

Interactive Map

Suggested Itinerary

Day 1: Royal Madrid

Morning:
  • Arrive in Madrid and check into your hotel
  • Visit the Royal Palace of Madrid and its beautiful gardens
Afternoon:
  • Explore the Plaza Mayor and surrounding streets
  • Visit the Almudena Cathedral next to the Royal Palace
Evening:
  • Enjoy tapas at Mercado San Miguel
  • Watch a flamenco show in the evening

Day 2: Art and Culture

Morning:
  • Visit the Prado Museum to see works by Goya, Velázquez, and El Greco
  • Stroll through the Botanical Garden nearby
Afternoon:
  • Explore the Reina Sofía Museum to see Picasso's Guernica
  • Visit the Thyssen-Bornemisza Museum to complete the "Art Triangle"
Evening:
  • Enjoy dinner in the lively La Latina neighborhood
  • Experience Madrid's nightlife in the Huertas area

Day 3: Parks and Departure

Morning:
  • Spend the morning at El Retiro Park, rowing on the lake
  • Visit the Palacio de Cristal glass palace in the park
Afternoon:
  • Last-minute shopping for souvenirs on Gran Vía
  • Enjoy a farewell lunch of authentic paella
