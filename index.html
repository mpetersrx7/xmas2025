Home / Amsterdam

Amsterdam

Explore the beautiful canals, museums, and vibrant neighborhoods of Amsterdam from December 13-17.

Points of Interest

  • Anne Frank House
    Historic house and museum dedicated to Jewish wartime diarist Anne Frank
  • Van Gogh Museum
    Art museum dedicated to the works of Vincent van Gogh and his contemporaries
  • Rijksmuseum
    Dutch national museum dedicated to arts and history in Amsterdam
  • Rembrandt House Museum
    Historic house and art museum where Rembrandt lived and worked
  • De Kas Restaurant
    Farm-to-table dining in a greenhouse setting
  • Zaanse Schans
    Neighborhood with historic windmills and houses
  • De Pijp Neighborhood
    Vibrant district with market, cafes, and boutiques
